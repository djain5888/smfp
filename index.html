About Us

As one of India's leading fully integrated steel producers, Shyam Metalics with its signature offering under brand umbrella of "S-E-L Tiger." is known for unmatched strength, precision and resilience. With an installed capacity of 13.50 MTPA and seven advanced plants, Shyam Metalics dominates the steel industry from ore to metal, controlling every step to ensure premium quality and cost efficiency.

At the heart of its operations are integrated manufacturing plants, efficient production systems and captive power plants, enabling quick adaptation to market shifts while maintaining consistent excellence. Just like a tiger, we move swiftly, strategically and with unbeatable power, setting new benchmarks in the steel industry.
